Portrait of Amalia Von Hohenberg

1859

A woman of exceptional precision, her immaculate attire and discipline accessories reflect a mind attuned to order. Her flame-red hair, secured with meticulous care, expresses the methodical brilliance that makes her indispensable to Herr Von Adlerberg. Not a detail escapes her watchful eyes.
